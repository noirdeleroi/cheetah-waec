Our Mission

At Cheetah WAEC, our mission is to empower students across West Africa with free, high-quality, and accessible tools to master mathematics. We are committed to supporting equitable education by providing a modern, interactive platform that simplifies learning, encourages practice, and builds confidence—especially for students preparing for the WAEC examination.

We believe every student deserves the opportunity to succeed, and we’re here to bridge the gap with engaging content, smart technology, and a supportive learning community.

We are building a community around WAEC preparation. If you are a passionate educator, tutor, or content creator and want to help students learn better, we would love to work with you.
