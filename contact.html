Our Mission

At Cheetah WAEC, our mission is to empower students across West Africa with free, high-quality, and accessible tools to master mathematics.

We are committed to supporting equitable education by providing a modern, interactive platform that simplifies learning, encourages practice, and builds confidence - especially for students preparing for the WAEC examination.

We are building a community around WAEC preparation. If you are a passionate educator, tutor, or content creator and want to help students learn better, we would love to work with you.
