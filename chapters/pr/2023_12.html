"Evaluate, correct to three decimal place \(\frac{4.314 \times 0.000056}{0.0067}\).
Possible answers:
A. \(0.037\)
B. \(0.004\)
C. \(0.361\)
D. \(0.036\)
Correct answer: D"

Solution.

To evaluate the expression \(\frac{4.314 \times 0.000056}{0.0067}\) and round it to three decimal places, we can follow these steps:
1. **Multiply 4.314 by 0.000056**:
- Convert the numbers to scientific notation: \(4.314 \times 10^0\) and \(5.6 \times 10^{-5}\).
- Multiply the coefficients: \(4.314 \times 5.6 = 24.1584\).
- Combine the powers of 10: \(10^0 \times 10^{-5} = 10^{-5}\).
- Result: \(24.1584 \times 10^{-5} = 0.000241584\).
2. **Divide the result by 0.0067**:
- Convert the numbers to scientific notation: \(2.41584 \times 10^{-4}\) and \(6.7 \times 10^{-3}\).
- Divide the coefficients: \(2.41584 \div 6.7 \approx 0.360573\).
- Combine the powers of 10: \(10^{-4} \div 10^{-3} = 10^{-1}\).
- Result: \(0.360573 \times 10^{-1} = 0.0360573\).
3. **Round to three decimal places**:
- The result \(0.0360573\) rounded to three decimal places is \(0.036\) because the fourth decimal place is 0, which is less than 5.
Thus, the final answer is \(\boxed{D}\).
