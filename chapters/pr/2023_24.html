"Arrange the following in ascending order of magnitude \(110_{\text {two }}, 31_{\text {eight }}, 42_{\text {five }}\).
Possible answers:
A. \(110_{\text {two }}, 31_{\text {five }}, 42_{\text {eight }}\)
B. \(42_{\text {five }}, 110_{\text {two }}, 31_{\text {eight }}\)
C. \(42_{\text {five }}, 31_{\text {eight }}, 110_{\text {two }}\)
D. \(110_{\text {two }}, 42_{\text {five }}, 31_{\text {eight }}\)
Correct answer: D"

Solution.

To arrange the numbers \(110_{\text{two}}\), \(31_{\text{eight}}\), and \(42_{\text{five}}\) in ascending order, we first convert them to base ten:
1. \(110_{\text{two}}\):
\[ \\ 1 \cdot 2^2 + 1 \cdot 2^1 + 0 \cdot 2^0 = 4 + 2 + 0 = 6_{10} \\ \]
2. \(31_{\text{eight}}\):
\[ \\ 3 \cdot 8^1 + 1 \cdot 8^0 = 24 + 1 = 25_{10} \\ \]
3. \(42_{\text{five}}\):
\[ \\ 4 \cdot 5^1 + 2 \cdot 5^0 = 20 + 2 = 22_{10} \\ \]
Converting these to decimal, we get \(6\), \(25\), and \(22\) respectively. Arranging these values in ascending order gives \(6\), \(22\), \(25\), which corresponds to \(110_{\text{two}}\), \(42_{\text{five}}\), \(31_{\text{eight}}\).
Thus, the correct answer is \(\boxed{D}\).
