Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how CheetahWAEC ("we", "us") handles information when you use cheetahwaec.com.

Last updated: February 6, 2026.

Who we are

CheetahWAEC is an independent WAEC exam preparation website. We provide study resources, past questions, solutions, and practice tools for students across West Africa.

What data we collect

1) Basic usage data

We may collect basic analytics information such as pages visited, approximate location (country/region), device/browser information, and referral sources. This helps us understand what content is useful and improve the website.

2) Contact information

If you contact us, we may receive your email address and the content of your message. We use this only to respond and provide support.

Cookies and advertising

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate the site and to display advertising.

Google AdSense: We may show ads served by Google AdSense. Google and third-party vendors may use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website and/or other websites.

  • Users can personalize ads in Google's Ads Settings.
  • Users can also opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

Note: Ad partners and vendors may have their own privacy policies. We do not control their cookies directly.

Analytics

We may use analytics tools (for example, Google Analytics or similar) to measure traffic and understand site performance. These tools may set cookies and collect usage information as described above.

Data retention

We keep contact messages for as long as needed to provide support, resolve issues, and maintain reasonable records. Analytics data is retained for a limited period according to the settings of our analytics providers.

Children's privacy

CheetahWAEC is designed for students and may be used by minors. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal data to us, please contact us and we will work to delete it.

Parents/guardians: please guide children on safe internet use and advise them not to share sensitive information.
