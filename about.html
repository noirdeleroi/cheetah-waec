About CheetahWAEC

CheetahWAEC helps students prepare for the WAEC exam with clear explanations, past questions, and practical practice.

Our mission

Our mission is to make WAEC preparation accessible, clear, and practical - especially for students who don't have access to expensive tutoring or paid materials.

What we offer

  • Study Guide: topic-by-topic notes with examples.
  • Past Papers: real WAEC-style questions to practice.
  • Practice mode: solve questions and get instant feedback.
  • Solutions & explanations: step-by-step help when you're stuck.

Transparency

CheetahWAEC is an independent resource and is not affiliated with WAEC (West African Examinations Council).

How to support

  • Share the site with friends or your class group.
  • Send feedback and report mistakes so we can fix them.
  • If you can, consider supporting the project on the donor page.
